GenderGander

(www.gender-gander.shinyapps.io/myapp/)


Gender inequality is a pressing social issue all around the globe. It is imperative for the general public to be cognizant of it, and match their perceptions of the extent of the problem to the reality. This app intends to raise awareness among the general public, as well as potentially provide insights to policymakers about relevant, influential variables that can help inform decision-making while attempting to combat the issue. This is an interactive app that seeks to answer the following questions:

  • In which countries is gender inequality most prevalent?
  • Is your perception of gender inequality aligned with the reality?
  • Is there any relationship between other the GGI and certain other indicators?
